Our Story

Welcome to HAJW — a small, passionate store dedicated to preserving and sharing the incredible weaving traditions of the Bodo community of Assam, Northeast India.

The Bodo people are renowned for their distinctive handloom textiles, woven with precision and pride on traditional looms passed down through generations. Our blouses are made by skilled artisans in local villages, ensuring every piece carries an authentic story.

We believe fashion can be personal and purposeful. That's why every product in our store is customizable — you can have your name or initials woven or embroidered directly into the fabric, making your garment truly one-of-a-kind.

By choosing HAJW, you support local weavers, celebrate indigenous craftsmanship, and wear a piece of living culture. Thank you for being part of this journey.
