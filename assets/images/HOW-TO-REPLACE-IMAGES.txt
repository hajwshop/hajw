HOW TO REPLACE BANNER IMAGES
==============================

Men's card (homepage):
  Replace: assets/images/men-banner.jpg
  Any JPG/PNG works. Recommended size: 900x700px or larger.

Women's card (homepage):
  Replace: assets/images/women-banner.jpg
  Any JPG/PNG works. Recommended size: 900x700px or larger.

Just drop your image file in this folder with the exact same filename.
You can also use a different filename — just update the src in index.html:
  <img src="assets/images/YOUR-FILE.jpg" ...>
